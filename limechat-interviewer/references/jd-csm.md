# Customer Success Manager (CSM) — LimeChat
Status: Active | Location: Bengaluru, Gurgaon

## What We're Looking For
A high-ownership CSM to own the post-onboarding journey for growth-stage clients.
Drive outcomes, not just answer tickets — shaping strategy, optimising campaigns,
and ensuring every account sees tangible business impact across revenue, automation, and CX.

## Responsibilities
- Own a portfolio of growth clients: account health, adoption, and satisfaction
- Drive measurable value: understand client goals (revenue, repeat purchase, support
  deflection, CSAT) and design strategies on LimeChat to hit them
- Run regular reviews: lead monthly/quarterly business reviews with performance,
  insights, and next steps
- Optimise journeys & campaigns: configure and iterate on WhatsApp flows, automation
  journeys, and campaigns to improve conversions and retention
- Be the product expert clients trust: educate on best practices, new features, use cases;
  proactively suggest what they *should* do next
- Monitor account health: track adoption, key metrics, and product usage; spot early
  risk signs and intervene before churn
- Coordinate problem-solving: partner with Support, Product, Engineering to resolve
  issues quickly and communicate clearly with clients
- Advocate for customers internally: bring structured feedback to Product & GTM teams
- Support expansion & renewals: work with Sales/Leadership on upsell, cross-sell, renewals

## Must-Haves
- 1–3 years in a customer-facing role (CS, Account Management, Onboarding) in B2B or SaaS
- Strong relationship-building and communication — can build trust with CX/Marketing leaders
- Solid problem-solving — can break down issues, find root causes, propose solutions
- Comfortable with data — pulling from dashboards/Sheets, interpreting trends, using in client convos
- Can manage multiple accounts and priorities — good with structure, follow-ups, documentation
- Basic comfort with technical concepts (APIs, integrations, events)
- High ownership, proactive client-first mindset — doesn't wait for clients to complain
- Comfortable in a fast-paced startup with evolving processes

## Nice-to-Have
- Experience managing D2C, ecommerce, or growth-stage brands
- Exposure to CX / Martech / CRM / support products
- Familiarity with WhatsApp marketing, conversational commerce, or chatbot/AI tools
- Experience running A/B tests or growth initiatives with clients

## Tools
Notion · Slack · Google Sheets/Docs · Freshdesk/HubSpot or similar · Analytics dashboards · Jira

## Growth Path
- Year 1: Manage growth accounts, deliver strong retention and NRR, build repeatable playbooks
- Year 2+: Senior CSM → Enterprise CSM / Onboarding / Product/Growth
