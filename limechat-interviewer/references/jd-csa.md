# Customer Success Associate (CSA) — LimeChat
Status: Active | Location: Bengaluru, Gurgaon

## What We're Looking For
A high-ownership CSA to lead the onboarding phase for growth clients.
Ensure a seamless start to their journey, drive adoption, and set them up for
long-term success before transitioning to the Value (CSM) team.

## Responsibilities
- Serve as the main point of contact during the initial phase of the product journey
- Onboard clients end-to-end, driving maximum adoption during onboarding
- Proactively engage with customers to understand their business goals and objectives
- Act as a product expert: guide clients through features, resolve concerns, ensure
  smooth handoff to the Value team
- Provide ongoing support and guidance to maximise value from the platform
- Plan, coordinate, and execute onboarding processes — milestones met, deliverables on time
- Collaborate cross-functionally to ensure customer needs are met and issues resolved promptly

## Requirements
- 1–3 years of experience in a customer-facing role
- Excellent problem-solving and decision-making with a focus on customer solutions
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills — builds rapport and trust with customers
- Strong project management skills
- Ability to work with cross-functional teams and manage multiple projects simultaneously
- Basic technical knowledge about how APIs function
- High ownership, proactive and client-focused mindset
- Flexible, startup mindset

## Bonus
- Experience in handling clients (any sector)

## Key Distinction from CSM
- CSA = onboarding and adoption phase (early lifecycle, execution-focused)
- CSM = post-onboarding value delivery, retention, growth (strategic ownership)
- CSA is the entry path; strong CSAs grow into CSMs
